Privacy Policy (Sort Of)

Welcome to Device Guard’s Privacy Policy, brewed fresh daily. Here, your privacy is as important to us as our morning coffee (which is to say, extremely important until about 11am).

Our (Absurd) Privacy Rules

  • ☕ We collect zero data before our first cup of coffee. If you visit before 8am, you are basically invisible to us.
  • ☕ If you spill coffee on your device, we promise to send you moral support and a virtual napkin.
  • ☕ All cookies on this site are strictly chocolate chip. No tracking, just snacking.
  • ☕ If you try to access our database without offering us a coffee, your request will be delayed until we finish our current cup.
  • ☕ We only share your data with highly trained coffee beans. They keep secrets better than we do.
  • ☕ If you read this policy backwards while drinking espresso, you unlock a secret achievement (but nothing actually happens).
  • ☕ Any personal information you provide will be encrypted, caffeinated, and possibly sprinkled with cinnamon.
  • ☕ If you unsubscribe, we’ll be sad, but we’ll drown our sorrows in a cappuccino and move on.

Questions?
