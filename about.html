Our Plans in Detail

Basic Plan
  • Essential protection for a single device (mobile, laptop, PC, or console).
  • Covers accidental drops, minor spills, and basic hardware failures.
  • Includes access to our support team during business hours.
  • Affordable monthly rate, ideal for students and everyday users.
  • Does not cover theft or advanced cyber threats.
Plus Plan
  • All Basic Plan features, plus coverage for theft and loss.
  • Priority support with faster response times.
  • Annual device health check and maintenance tips.
  • Perfect for professionals and families who want extra peace of mind.
Premium Plan
  • All Plus Plan features, plus coverage for multiple devices (up to 3 per user).
  • Includes accidental damage from liquids, power surges, and screen breakage.
  • 24/7 rapid response and device replacement within 48 hours.
  • Cybersecurity suite: antivirus, anti-phishing, and data recovery assistance.
  • Recommended for families, remote workers, and gamers with several devices.
Ultra Plan
  • All Premium Plan features, plus unlimited device coverage for your household or business.
  • Advanced cyber protection: VPN, identity theft monitoring, and secure cloud backup.
  • Personalized support with a dedicated Device Guard agent.
  • On-site technical assistance (where available).
  • Best for businesses, tech enthusiasts, and anyone who wants the ultimate in device security.
